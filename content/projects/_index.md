After completing my bachelor thesis, I looked back and saw just how much I had learned, but also realized just how little I had created in my own free time. The study load and a full time position had sucked every ounce of my creative energy out of me.

So I decided to make a change. I quit my job, and decided to take some time off to focus on studies and my own projects. To fall in love back again with tech, create and tinker with code just like I used to before, and learn along the way.

Below is a display of some of these projects, big or small they each taught me something valuable, and I hope you enjoy reading about them.

What might stand out to you is their naming. I've taken a bit of inspiration from military codenames, and given each project a codename related in some way to things I'm interested in. I've always found it cool, it sounds bit more mysterious and captivating than just "CNN" or "AI Agent", although I will admit it does tank the clarity a bit.
